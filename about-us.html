About Us


The Review is the official student publication of Northwestern University. The Review is guided by the principles of responsible campus journalism, motivated by the Core Values of Northwestern University, and shall go on and unflinchingly fight for freedom of the press and the right of the students .The publication releases its regular issues in various formats including tabloids, newsletters, and magazines. Its literary folio, Flame, is also published every year.
